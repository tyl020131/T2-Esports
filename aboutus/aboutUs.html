ABOUT US

G1 Tech is a retail store that is dedicated into selling computers, gadgets and accessories. We started our business on 1st of April 2018. We are officially registered and certified by Suruhanjaya Syarikat Malaysia (SSM). We are committed to provide our consumers with the best shopping experience be it at our physical G1 Tech store or on our newly developed online store.

Here in G1 Tech, we fully understand the needs and requirements of our customers. At the same time, our company is always keeping updated with newly released products by various company and will try to get the supplies from them as soon as possible. With that being said, we will always try our best to offer our highly valued customers with the latest and best products available at this fast paced modern technology world.

Our company had properly planned our aims before we started this business. We have also analyze and chose the best suppliers who supplies us with products which are widely known and also leading brands in the world. Therefore, the products that we sell to our customers will always be top notch. Throughout the whole time our business has been running, we have achieve some achievements that we are proud of.
